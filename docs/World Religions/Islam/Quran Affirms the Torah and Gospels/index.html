The Quran Affirms the Bible

Published on: February 2, 2025

Confirmed Earlier Sources​

The Quran consistently upholds the continuity of divine revelation, affirming that it does not introduce a new message but rather confirms previous scriptures, particularly the Torah (Tawrah) and the Gospel (Injeel). It acknowledges these earlier revelations as part of the same divine guidance, urging the People of the Book—Jews and Christians—to recognize the Quran as a continuation of God’s message. The Quran emphasizes that true belief entails accepting all of God’s revelations and warns against rejecting the message of previous scriptures.

The following Quran Verses confirm and validate the previous scriptures. These verses clearly show that the Torah and the Gospels cannot have been corrupted and that Allah himself handed them down.
