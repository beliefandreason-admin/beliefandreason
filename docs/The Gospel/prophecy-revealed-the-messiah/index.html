Messianic Prophecies: Old Testament

Published on: February 9, 2025

Comprehensive List of Messianic Prophecies from Genesis to Revelation

Introduction to Messianic Prophecy​

Messianic prophecy refers to the biblical predictions concerning the coming of the Messiah, a savior or anointed one who would bring redemption and establish God's kingdom. These prophecies are found in the Hebrew Bible (Old Testament).

Christ is the fulfillment of these prophecies, as affirmed in the New Testament, where His life, death, and resurrection are seen as the realization of the promises made in the Old Testament.
