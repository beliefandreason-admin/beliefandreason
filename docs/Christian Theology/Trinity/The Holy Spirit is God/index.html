ðŸ•Š Key Insightsâ€‹

These verses make it clear that the Holy Spirit is fully God:

  1. He is called God directly (Acts 5:3-4, 2 Corinthians 3:17).
  2. He has the attributes of God (Eternal: Hebrews 9:14, Omniscient: 1 Corinthians 3. 2:10-11, Omnipresent: Psalm 139:7-8, etc.).
  3. He is worshiped as God (Matthew 28:19, 2 Corinthians 13:14, Philippians 3:3).
  4. He forgives sins (which only God can do) (Hebrews 10:15-17, Romans 8:2).
  5. He created all things (Genesis 1:2, Job 33:4).
  6. He calls Himself God (Isaiah 63:10, Hebrews 10:15-17).
  7. He is the final Judge (Acts 5:3-4, John 16:8).
